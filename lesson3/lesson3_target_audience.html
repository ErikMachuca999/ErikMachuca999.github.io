Target Audience for the weather site

Who is this Website for?

The website is for anyone! for Tavelers just passing through, or visitors
coming to sightsee and stay awhile, to even fellow Prestonites, to even
just people curious about the weather here. This Site is for sharing to all
about the weather here in Preston, Idaho and can be used for their advantage
or learning.

Two Personas:

Jim:
I am a wanderer. I've been around more than 1 block. I tell ya, there are places
I've gone to, and really wished I had an umbrella or coat. A nice Heads up would
spare me some grumbles and groans. And this is perfect as I travel through Preston,
and sometimes end up staying for a time.

Lydia:
I live in Preston, and I have a family farm of Apple Orchards and Many Veggies, and Livestock.
I definetly would love to plan for every day being more easier. I Hate unexpected situations.
I would love seeing ahead if there are gonna be good weeks of rain to not goof up plant watering,
or seeing if cold air is coming in a few short hours, so that the land don't suffer.

Scenarios:

1: Travelers from Washington, or Oregon, or Montana, or even Idaho needing to go to Utah or Wyoming, and
their route takes them through Preston. Wanting to know how the weather is when staying for the night.

2: Tourists coming to the locations Preston shares with the world, and they are unaware of "slight" changes
to the temperature and instead of knowing it'll be sunny, the temperature will drop about 35 degrees.

3: A Student looking for quick and easily readable weather predictions for a comparing climate project of
Idaho Cities.

4: Farmers in need of weather predictions to tend to their land's protection.

5: Web surfers who jsut want to know what the temperature is in different parts of the continent.
